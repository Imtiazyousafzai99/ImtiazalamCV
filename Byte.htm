World War II: Causes, Major Events, and Lasting Impact

World War II was one of the most significant events in global history, lasting from 1939 to 1945 and involving almost every major world power. The conflict fundamentally reshaped political boundaries, international relations, and national economies. This article explores the causes, major battles, and the lasting impacts of the war.
